Ni Youtube video gallery is a plugin for wordpress that allows you to embed youtube video gallery into your website or blog.

== Description ==

<p>
Ni YouTube Video Gallery is a powerful plugin designed specifically for WordPress websites. It offers the functionality to seamlessly embed a gallery of YouTube videos into your website or blog. With this plugin, you can showcase a collection of your favorite or relevant videos directly on your site, enhancing the visual appeal and engagement for your visitors
</p>

<p>
This plugin is user-friendly and easy to install, making it suitable for both beginners and experienced WordPress users. It provides a straightforward and intuitive interface that allows you to customize and configure your video gallery according to your preferences. You can choose from various layout options, adjust the number of videos displayed per page, and even apply filters or categories to organize your videos effectively.
</p>

== Installation ==

1. Use the WordPress plugin installer or upload files to WordPress plugin directory.
1. Activate plugin from admin panel.
1. Copy youtube video URL 
1. Add New Video, Select Category.
1. Create new Page and Select Template.
1. Add Page to menu.
1. Display video on front end.
